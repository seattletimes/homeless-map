Where are people homeless in King County?

The Jan. 26, 2018 Point in Time count of homelessness in King County found 12,112 people, up 4 percet from the year before. But the count found much larger increases in some areas.

To discover stories about the homelessness crisis is neighborhoods across the county, tap on census tracts outlined in black.
